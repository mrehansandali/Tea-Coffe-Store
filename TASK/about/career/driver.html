Driver Position at Tea & Coffee Store

About Us

Tea & Coffee Store is a well-established retailer specializing in high-quality teas, coffees, and delicious cookies. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction is at the heart of our business.

Job Description

We are seeking a reliable and experienced driver to join our team. As a driver at Tea & Coffee Store, you will play a crucial role in ensuring the timely and safe delivery of our products to our customers. Your responsibilities will include:

  • Transporting our products to customers' locations promptly and safely
  • Verifying orders and ensuring accuracy in delivery
  • Maintaining the delivery vehicle in good working condition
  • Providing excellent customer service during deliveries
  • Adhering to all traffic laws and safety regulations
  • Assisting with loading and unloading products when needed

Qualifications

The ideal candidate for this position should possess the following qualifications:

  • A valid driver's license
  • Clean driving record
  • Experience in a delivery driver role
  • Good knowledge of the local area and routes
  • Strong attention to detail and accuracy
  • Excellent customer service skills

How to Apply

If you meet the qualifications and are interested in joining our team as a driver, please send your resume and a cover letter to [email address]. In your cover letter, please specify why you're interested in this position and how your skills and experience make you a suitable candidate.

We appreciate your interest in Tea & Coffee Store and look forward to potentially welcoming you as a valuable member of our team.
